Run an AI workforce the way you'd run a company — with goals, an org chart,

---

## The problem

Most enterprise AI pilots stall after the demo. Individuals use ChatGPT and
Copilot, but no one owns *the workforce*. There's no goal hierarchy, no
budget enforcement, no audit log, and no way for a CFO or risk officer to
answer "what did the agents actually do, and what did they cost us?"

AgentDash is the management layer between your business and your agents.

## What we do

Two arms working together. The **platform** is the agent control plane —
it's where you define companies, hire agents, set goals, enforce budgets,
and review activity. The **consulting practice** installs that platform
inside enterprises: we run a structured deployment in 8–10 weeks, not a
slideware engagement.

## How we work with you

Two ways to engage. Both start small and grow with trust.

### Pilot — 4 to 6 weeks, fixed scope, fixed price
We diagnose the highest-impact pain points, design the agent org, ship the
first agents into production with board oversight wired in from day one.
Goal: prove the workforce shipping real work in production.

### Production — Quarterly retainer, expanding scope
Embedded with your team for the first quarter of operation. Weekly review,
expanding scope, eventual handoff to your team. Goal: build the operating
muscle to run agents long-term without us.

## Why this works

**Start small, prove value fast, expand with trust.** We don't try to
automate the whole company on day one. We find the two or three highest-
impact pain points, deploy agents in two weeks, and grow from there.

**The LLM tokens are yours.** We charge for orchestration, not inference.
Bring your own Anthropic / OpenAI / open-source endpoint. We'll deploy
agents into your air-gapped environment if that's what compliance requires.

**Agents work alongside humans, not in a separate silo.** Every agent has
a manager (human or AI), every task has a parent, every decision is
reviewable. The org chart is a real org chart.
